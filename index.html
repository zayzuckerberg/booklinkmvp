Protect Your Time and Money

There is nothing more annoying than setting up an appointment with a client,
agreeing on a date and time, preparing for the meeting just for them not to
show up, call or even reschedule. You lose money but more importantly you lose
time that you can never gain back. Thankfully, we’ve came up with a solution.

How it works

  1. Create an account
  2. Create a Booklink with your clients details
  3. Enter your cancellation and delay timeframe
  4. Enter your no show fee amount
  5. Send the link to the client

Boom! that’s it.

Once the client has confirmed and paid the fee, you'll recieve confirmation.
If they show up they get their money back. If they no show you get the money!
